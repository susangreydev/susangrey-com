Susan Grey is a seasoned solo vocalist & choral performer.

A few of her fondest performance opportunities include having served as official National Anthem singer for the U.S. Air Force overseas and here at home, giving an array of regular performances for senior citizen programs in Miami, and portraying Mary in Harmony of Faith's first annual production of Lamb of God (Tampa, 2024).

Susan loves to perform sets of different genres of music such as greatest hits, pop favorites, Oldies, religious, and Christmas.

She is also available to perform the National Anthem at any venue in the Tampa Bay area.
